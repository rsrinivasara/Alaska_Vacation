Packing Essentials

For Alaska in August with kids

🧥
Layers for everyone
Temps range 40–65°F. Fleece + waterproof shell per person. Glaciers and the Denali bus are much colder.
🌧️
Rain gear
Light waterproof jacket for every person. August showers especially on the Kenai Peninsula.
🔭
Binoculars
Essential for Denali bus and Kenai Fjords boat. One pair per family minimum.
💊
Dramamine / Sea-Bands
For the Kenai Fjords boat (choppy outer waters) and the Denali park bus (unpaved road).
👟
Waterproof hiking shoes
For all adults and kids. Mandatory for Matanuska Glacier — closed-toe waterproof required.
🐻
Bear spray
Rentable at Denali. Worth having for trail hiking. Rangers will show you how to use it.
🧺
Large cooler + snacks
Stock before Denali — food is limited and expensive in the park. Pack lunches for the bus tour.
📷
Camera + battery bank
You will take more photos than you expect. A portable battery bank is essential for all-day outings.
🦟
Bug spray
Especially for Exit Glacier and forested trails. Alaska mosquitoes in August are legendary.
